A Series of Main Events

As we are all busy gentle women, I propose the following outline fore a crafting schedule.

Each session shall be no more brief than two hours, and no longer than three. Each shall be a focused crafting gathering, for to take advantage of the tools and expertise that each of us may not individually posess.

Sewing our Smocks

When

TBD

Where

TBD - Stephanie's crafting space?

Goal of Session

Start with the foundations, in every sense! Draft patterns (or use pre made patterns), cut smock pieces, understand construction and learn any necessary (historical or modern) sewing techniques. If time, baste pattern to check fit.

Whether using a premade pattern or drafting our own, let's take our measurements this session. They will be very useful for creating the rest of our costumes.

Before the Session

  • Purchase fabric, approximately 2-2.5 yards of white or off white linen, cotton, or blend
  • Pre-wash/preshrink said fabric

Bring

  • Pattern or example of style
  • 1.75-2.5 yds of PRE-SHRUNK white or off white linen or cotton fabric
  • Thread to match
  • Sewing supplies - Stephanie has many supplies and much equiptment, but if you have any you'd like to bring to reduce the need for sharing scarce resources feel free
